SMART Cell OHS Policy

SMART Cell is dedicated to creating a safe and healthy work environment by adhering to international standards like ISO 9001:2015, ISO 45001:2018, and local labor laws, while also meeting sustainability benchmarks such as the Eco Vadis evaluation.

We ensure all employees receive thorough OHS training from qualified third parties and continuous safety orientations to maintain high competence and adherence to safety protocols. Our workforce is actively involved in identifying, reporting, and addressing safety concerns through dedicated communication channels and elected safety committees.

In addition, employees are empowered to stop operations when risks are identified, supported by the "Penalty and Rewards rules" to ensure incident prevention and performance control. SMART Cell aims for "zero incidents" and "100% compliance" by setting measurable safety goals and promoting continuous improvement.

We are committed to exceeding all legal health and safety requirements for the benefit of both our employees and the community.

SMART Cell Quality Policy

At SMART Cell, our Quality Policy drives us forward, ensuring that we remain a trusted leader in telecommunications services, known for our unwavering commitment to excellence and customer satisfaction.
